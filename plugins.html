What I've Learned

So far the readings have been about the history of social networks, what they are and how they have changed the way we communicate throughout the world. One of our readings did a real good job to define what social networking sites are by stating that there are three services that should be provided to the user of a social network site. These services allow users to; construct a profile within the system, articulate a list of other users with whom they share a connection, and lastly allow users to view and traverse their list of connections. The readings also discuss how social networks have changed how we share information. One common tool used to share information are social plugins, a link posted on websites that allow users to share and post information on their social profiles.
